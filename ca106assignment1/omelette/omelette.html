Omlette

Ingrediets Required:

  • Eggs x 3
  • Bacon Rashers x 2
  • Onion x 1
  • Cheese (50g)
  • Cooking Oil

Method:

  1. Pour some cooking oil on the pan to preheat the oil.
  2. Chop up the bacon and the onion into small pieces.
  3. Begin by frying the bacon, shortly after it is fried put onions in with the bacon.
  4. Crack the eggs separetly into a cup and wisk them.
  5. When the bacon and onions are ready add the egg mix into the pan.
  6. After you see the egg mix solidify add cheese.
  7. When you begin to see that the omelette is beginning to darken remove it from the pan and serve.

Enjoy your meal!
