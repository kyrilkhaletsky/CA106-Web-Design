Pancakes

Ingrediets Required:

  • Eggs x 1
  • Milk (300ml)
  • Butter (1 tbsp.)
  • Plain Flour (100g)
  • Sugar (10g)

Method:

  1. Add egg, Milk, flour and sugar into a mixing bowl and mix until the solution is uniform.
  2. Preheat the pan and add butter for lubrication.
  3. Add pancake mix into the pan until the base is covered in pancake mix.
  4. When the mixture begins to solidify flip the pancake.
  5. After both sides are done proceed to repeating for the rest of the pancake mix.

Enjoy your meal!
